# **App Name**: Mwijay Davie: Creative Technologist

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Project Showcase: Present detailed portfolio projects with descriptions, images, tech stacks, and relevant metrics or impact.
- Skills and Expertise Display: Organize and showcase technical, AI, creative, and business skills with visual indicators or categories.
- Professional About Section: Feature a compelling bio, unique value proposition, fun facts, and a professional photo to build brand identity.
- Interactive Contact & Collaboration Form: Provide a contact form for inquiries, job opportunities, or collaborations, with client-side validation and specific subject options.
- Resume Download: Allow visitors to easily download Mwijay Davie's CV in an ATS-optimized PDF format.
- AI-Powered Project Matchmaker: An interactive tool where users can describe their needs or ask questions, and the AI intelligently suggests relevant projects or skill sets from Mwijay's portfolio.
- Dark/Light Mode Toggle: Enable users to switch between light and dark display modes, respecting system preferences and saving their choice.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a striking Electric Blue (#2563EB), used for call-to-actions and key elements, suggesting professionalism and technology. The accent color is a vibrant Success Green (#10B981) for highlights and positive indicators, reflecting growth and innovation. The palette provides dedicated light mode backgrounds of white (#FFFFFF) and light grey surfaces (#F8FAFC), as well as a cohesive dark mode with a dark blue-grey background (#0F172A) and deeper grey surfaces (#1E293B).
- Headings ('--font-display') will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy feel, while body text ('--font-body') will be 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring readability and a contemporary aesthetic across the site. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Lightweight, clean icons will be sourced from either Lucide Icons or Phosphor Icons, maintaining a crisp and modern visual appeal.
- The site will adhere to a mobile-first responsive design, transitioning to multi-column layouts on larger screens. Sections like Hero and About will use a two-column desktop layout, stacking on mobile. Projects will feature a prominent lead project followed by a responsive grid.
- Sophisticated animations will be driven by CSS and the Intersection Observer API. Scroll animations will smoothly fade in and subtly move sections, skill bars will fill, and project cards will stagger their appearance. Hover effects will include slight scaling and shadow increases for interactive elements, and micro-interactions like theme toggles will feature smooth transitions and icon rotations. A progress bar will indicate scroll depth at the top of the page.
